AI-Powered Interactive Learning & Visual Knowledge Assistant
Project Brief

The AI-Powered Interactive Learning & Visual Knowledge Assistant is an intelligent educational web application designed to make 
learning more engaging through conversational AI, voice interaction, and real-time visual content. Users can ask questions using 
either text or voice, and the system responds through a realistic AI avatar that speaks naturally in English, Hindi, or Telugu 
while maintaining conversational context. Simultaneously, the right side of the interface presents structured learning content in 
English, including concise explanations, key points, high-quality images, examples, comparison tables, and interesting facts. 
By combining Large Language Models (LLMs), speech technologies, AI avatars, and image retrieval APIs, the platform creates an 
immersive learning experience that goes beyond traditional chatbots. This MVP focuses on delivering a clean, responsive, and 
visually engaging prototype without user authentication or databases, making it lightweight, easy to deploy, and ideal for 
demonstrations, portfolios and future expansion.
